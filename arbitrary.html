Example Page

Pretty much everything on this page can be changed on the fly using an external file.

Want to hide this text? One click and it's gone!

That's right, just click that big button down there and it's gone.

  • Push the button marked, "Show Image Grid". It'll do exactly what it says.
  • Once you see the grid, you can click and drag all the images around the page pretty much wherever you want.
  • Want a better view of one of the images? That's pretty easy to do, too. Just double-click it and it'll grow to fill the screen. When you're done viewing it at high-resolution, just double-click it again and it'll collapse right back down to its original location on the original grid.
  • Want to randomize the placement of the pictures on the grid? That's pretty easy, too. Just click "Show Image Grid" again, and it'll show a new, randomized grid of pictures.
